Case 13 — Smart Residential Surveillance Pods

Brief Description

Smart Residential Surveillance Pods monitor entry points in housing areas using cameras and sensors, detect anomalies, and alert security teams instantly.

Rising Problems

  • Unauthorized access to residential areas.
  • Manual visitor verification delays.
  • No detection of suspicious behavior patterns.
  • Slow communication with patrol teams.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Stores resident and visitor records.
  • KMP: Detects repeated suspicious patterns.
  • Queue: Manages entry and exit logs.
  • Dijkstra: Routes patrol units quickly.

Improves neighborhood safety.

Creates secure living environments.

Case 8 — Smart Emergency Beacon System

Brief Description

The Smart Emergency Beacon System is a city-wide safety mechanism designed to guide citizens during emergencies such as fires, floods, accidents, or natural disasters. Beacon poles equipped with lights, sirens, and digital signals activate automatically to direct people toward safe zones.

Rising Problems

  • Citizens panicking due to lack of clear evacuation guidance.
  • Delayed emergency alerts reaching affected areas.
  • Uncoordinated evacuation causing congestion and confusion.
  • Failure of manual warning systems during large-scale emergencies.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • BFS (Breadth-First Search): Propagates emergency signals across beacon nodes rapidly.
  • Hash Table: Stores beacon status, signal strength, and operational health.
  • Queue: Manages multiple alert broadcasts in the correct sequence.
  • Graph Traversal: Determines safest evacuation paths dynamically.

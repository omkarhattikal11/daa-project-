Flood & Water-Level Monitoring System

1 • Problem Statement & Solution Overview

Problem Statement:

  • Floods occur due to heavy rainfall, overflow of rivers/dams, and poor drainage. Most areas lack real-time monitoring, so warnings come too late.

Solution Overview:

  • Measure water height in rivers, lakes, dams, and drainage channels.
  • Track rainfall intensity and predict rising water levels.
  • Send real-time data to a central server or cloud.
  • Analyze water data and give alerts

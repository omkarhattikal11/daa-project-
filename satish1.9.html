Case 9 — AI-Powered Lost Person Detection

Brief Description

The AI-Powered Lost Person Detection system assists authorities in locating missing individuals by analyzing CCTV footage across Velora. Using pattern-matching and intelligent search techniques, the system automatically scans camera feeds and flags possible matches in real time.

Rising Problems

  • Manual CCTV monitoring is slow and prone to human error.
  • Difficulty tracking a person across multiple locations and camera zones.
  • Delayed response time in critical missing-person cases.
  • Lack of a unified system to correlate sightings city-wide.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • KMP Algorithm: Matches facial feature patterns efficiently across video frames.
  • Hash Map: Stores sightings indexed by camera ID and timestamp.
  • Priority Queue: Ranks detected matches based on confidence score.
  • BFS: Searches nearby camera networks to predict movement direction.

Climate-Resilient City Resource Management System

1 • Problem Statement & Solution Overview

Problem Statement:

  • Water scarcity due to unpredictable rainfall & overuse.
  • Energy overload during heatwaves causing blackouts.
  • Floods & disasters overwhelming emergency systems.
  • Waste overflow due to inefficient route planning.
  • Pollution & heat island effect increasing health risks.
  • Unplanned urban expansion reducing climate resilience.

Solution Overview:

  • Tracks live water, energy, waste, pollution levels.
  • Uses AI + data structures for predictions.
  • Automatically optimizes resource distribution.
  • Uses graph algorithms for best routes.
  • Generates emergency alerts & evacuation plans.
  • Provides city dashboard for decision-making.

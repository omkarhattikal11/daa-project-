Case 6 — Emergency Coordination Tower

Brief Description

The Emergency Coordination Tower acts as Velora’s central hub for managing all emergency services including fire response, medical assistance, police operations, and disaster relief. It enables real-time coordination and optimized deployment of emergency units across the city.

Rising Problems

  • Delayed response due to lack of centralized emergency coordination.
  • Difficulty identifying the nearest available emergency unit.
  • Poor communication between fire, police, and medical departments.
  • Inefficient routing causing delays during critical situations.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Priority Queue: Classifies emergency requests based on severity.
  • Hash Table: Maintains real-time availability of emergency units.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Computes fastest routes for responders.
  • Queue: Manages sequential dispatch of emergency operations.

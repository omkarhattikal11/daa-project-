Case 3 — Smart Parking & Mobility Hub

Brief Description

The Smart Parking & Mobility Hub provides real-time parking slot availability, EV charging coordination, and last-mile transport connectivity in Velora. It integrates sensors and routing logic to reduce congestion and improve urban mobility efficiency.

Rising Problems

  • Drivers wasting time searching for available parking slots.
  • Traffic congestion near parking zones during peak hours.
  • Inefficient usage of EV charging infrastructure.
  • Lack of coordination between parking and local mobility services.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Maintains real-time mapping of vehicles to parking slots.
  • Min-Heap: Identifies the nearest available parking slot quickly.
  • BFS: Scans parking zones level-by-level for free spaces.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Calculates optimal routes for mobility shuttles.

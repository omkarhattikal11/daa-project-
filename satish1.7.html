Case 7 — Visitors Flow Analysis

Brief Description

The Visitors Flow Analysis system monitors and analyzes the movement of people across Velora’s public spaces such as parks, beaches, malls, event zones, and transit hubs. It helps city planners predict congestion, manage crowd safety, and improve visitor experience.

Rising Problems

  • Overcrowding in popular public areas during peak hours.
  • Safety risks caused by uncontrolled crowd movement.
  • Difficulty predicting visitor surges during festivals and events.
  • Inefficient deployment of security and support staff.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Sliding Window Technique: Continuously analyzes footfall data to detect sudden crowd spikes.
  • Hash Map: Stores real-time sensor data for each location.
  • BFS: Tracks how crowds spread across connected zones.
  • Priority Queue: Identifies high-risk congestion zones first.

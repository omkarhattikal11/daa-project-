Case 2 — Smart Governance & Citizen Service Centre

Brief Description

The Smart Governance & Citizen Service Centre provides a single digital platform for citizens to access government services such as certificates, tax payments, grievance registration, and service tracking. It reduces manual paperwork and improves transparency by digitizing governance workflows.

Rising Problems

  • Long waiting times at government service counters.
  • Manual record handling leading to errors and data loss.
  • Difficulty prioritizing elderly, disabled, or emergency cases.
  • Lack of transparency in application and grievance status.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Stores and retrieves citizen records and service requests efficiently.
  • Queue: Manages token-based service flow in an orderly manner.
  • Priority Queue (Heap): Gives higher priority to urgent and special-category requests.
  • Search Trees: Enables fast lookup of documents and historical records.

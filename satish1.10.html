Case 10 — Central Mall Management

Brief Description

The Central Mall Management system automates operations within Velora’s main shopping complex. It manages shop information, billing queues, indoor navigation, footfall analytics, and inventory alerts to enhance shopper convenience and optimize mall administration.

Rising Problems

  • Long waiting times at billing counters during peak shopping hours.
  • Difficulty for customers to locate specific shops or products.
  • Uneven crowd distribution across different floors and zones.
  • Delayed identification of low-stock or high-demand items.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Map: Stores shop details, product availability, and tenant information.
  • Queue: Manages customer flow at billing and checkout counters.
  • Priority Queue: Identifies high-demand products and busiest zones.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Computes shortest indoor navigation routes for customers.

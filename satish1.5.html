Case 5 — Central Security & Smart Surveillance Room

Brief Description

The Central Security & Smart Surveillance Room serves as Velora’s main command center for city-wide safety. It continuously monitors CCTV feeds, motion sensors, fire alarms, and emergency alerts, enabling real-time threat detection and rapid security response.

Rising Problems

  • Manual monitoring of multiple camera feeds causing delayed threat detection.
  • Difficulty prioritizing critical alerts during peak activity.
  • Lack of coordination between surveillance zones.
  • Slow deployment of security personnel to incident locations.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Stores and retrieves camera IDs, alert logs, and sensor data efficiently.
  • Priority Queue: Ranks alerts based on severity for immediate action.
  • BFS: Scans nearby cameras to track incident spread or movement.
  • AVL Tree: Maintains time-ordered incident records for fast querying.

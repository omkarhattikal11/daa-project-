Case 4 — City Info–Tourism Centre

Brief Description

The City Info–Tourism Centre acts as a digital guide for visitors exploring Velora. It provides real-time information about tourist attractions, crowd density, transport options, event schedules, and personalized travel routes to enhance visitor experience.

Rising Problems

  • Tourists struggling to find accurate information about city attractions.
  • Overcrowding at popular tourist spots during peak hours.
  • Inefficient travel routes between multiple attractions.
  • Slow manual search of attraction details.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Stores attraction details for quick information access.
  • Priority Queue (Heap): Ranks attractions based on popularity and crowd level.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Computes shortest and optimized tourist routes.
  • Trie: Enables fast search of attraction names and keywords.

bank and government queue management

1 • Problem Statement & Solution Overview

Problem Statement:

  • Long Wait Times: Customers often wait for extended periods due to inefficient queue management.
  • Priority Handling: Elderly, pregnant women, and VIP customers need priority service.
  • Multiple Service Counters: Customers need routing to the correct counter for their needs.
  • Dynamic Queue Changes: Some customers may leave or shift to another service.
  • Data Tracking: Difficulty in maintaining records of serviced customers and average wait time.

Solution Overview:

  • Supports multiple queues for different service types.
  • Uses priority queues to serve elderly, pregnant women, and VIPs first.
  • Tracks average waiting time and number of served customers.
  • Allows customers to change queue or leave dynamically.

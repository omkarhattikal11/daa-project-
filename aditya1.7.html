emergency alert system

1 • Problem Statement & Solution Overview

Problem Statement:

  • Delayed emergency communication leads to loss of life during fire, earthquake, chemical leaks, or medical emergencies.
  • Overloaded emergency lines cause critical alerts to be missed.
  • No automated evacuation guidance, people do not know which route is safe.
  • Lack of priority handling, minor alerts sometimes delay life-critical alerts.
  • Infrastructure systems (hospitals, smart cities) fail to coordinate emergency messages fast enough.

Solution Overview:

  • Detects emergency keywords from sensor logs using KMP.
  • Uses Hash Maps for quick emergency type lookup.
  • Uses a Max-Heap to prioritize life-threatening alerts.
  • Uses BFS to find safe reachable zones.
  • Uses Dijkstra to find fastest/safest evacuation path.
  • Uses Queue to broadcast notifications in FIFO order.
  • Ensures instant, reliable emergency communication.

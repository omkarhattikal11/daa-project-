Case 1 — Digital Advertising & Events Hub

Brief Description

The Digital Advertising & Events Hub manages real-time event operations at Velora’s central plaza. It handles advertisement scheduling, stall bookings, live crowd updates, and automated drone-based display routes to ensure smooth functioning during large public events.

Rising Problems

  • Clashing advertisement slot bookings during peak hours.
  • Difficulty managing stall allocation during large-scale events.
  • Inefficient crowd monitoring affecting event safety.
  • Drone display routes getting congested due to poor planning.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Allows fast lookup and management of advertiser and stall details.
  • Priority Queue (Heap): Assigns ad slots efficiently based on priority flow.
  • Queue: Used to rotate advertisements in a scheduled cycle.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Determines shortest and safest drone display route.

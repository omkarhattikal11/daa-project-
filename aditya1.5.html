Smart Parking Management System

1 • Problem Statement & Solution Overview

Problem Statement:

  • Cars circling the parking lot, wasting time and fuel.
  • Uneven utilization of parking spaces across floors/zones.
  • Difficulty finding parking for VIPs, handicapped, or reserved users.
  • No real-time tracking of available parking slots.
  • Manual management of parking leads to errors, delays, and inefficient usage.

Solution Overview:

  • Real-Time Resource Tracking
  • Emergency Triage.
  • Smart Ambulance Allocation
  • Optimize bus and train schedules dynamically using real-time data.
  • Predictive Resource Allocation
  • Central dashboard

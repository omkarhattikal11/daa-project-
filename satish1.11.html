Case 11 — City Post Office Control System

Brief Description

The City Post Office Control System manages parcel sorting, priority deliveries, route optimization, and real-time package tracking across Velora. It improves efficiency, reliability, and transparency in city-wide postal services.

Rising Problems

  • Manual parcel sorting causing delivery delays.
  • Urgent parcels not being prioritized properly.
  • High fuel consumption due to inefficient delivery routes.
  • Difficulty tracking parcel status in real time.

Solutions / Logic Used

  • Hash Table: Stores sender, receiver, and parcel tracking details.
  • Queue: Handles regular parcel processing.
  • Priority Queue: Ensures urgent and express parcels are dispatched first.
  • Dijkstra’s Algorithm: Determines shortest delivery routes.

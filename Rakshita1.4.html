Business Case 4: Restaurant Crowd & Demand Prediction System

1. Business Case Description

Restaurants in busy city areas often experience unpredictable customer footfall, especially during weekends, festivals, or special events. This makes it difficult to plan staff schedules, food preparation, and seating capacity efficiently.

The Restaurant Crowd & Demand Prediction System tracks real-time customer entries, analyzes historical footfall data, and predicts upcoming peak hours. This helps restaurants reduce waiting time, avoid food shortages, and improve overall customer satisfaction.

2. Problem Statement

  • Sudden overcrowding during peak hours
  • Under-preparation during unexpected rush periods
  • Manual analysis of historical data is inefficient
  • Poor planning of staff and resources

System Requirements

  • Continuously track customer entries
  • Store and update crowd data efficiently
  • Compare past and present footfall trends
  • Predict upcoming rush or off-peak periods

3. Solution Approach

  • Queue tracks customers entering the restaurant
  • Hash Map stores crowd count for each time slot
  • Binary Search Tree organizes historical footfall data
  • Arrays analyze time-based trends for prediction

System Output

  • Predicted peak and off-peak hours
  • Staffing and seating recommendations
  • Food preparation demand estimates

4. Algorithms and Data Structures Used

Queue — Tracking Customer Entry

Stores customers in the order they arrive.
Helps monitor live crowd size and waiting time.

Hash Map — Fast Footfall Storage

Stores customer count for each time slot or day.
Provides O(1) average-time access for trend analysis.

Binary Search Tree (BST) — Historical Trend Comparison

Stores past crowd values in sorted order.
Enables fast searching of peak-hour patterns.

Arrays with Linear Trend Logic — Demand Prediction

Stores time-series crowd data in arrays.
Detects increasing or decreasing trends to forecast demand.

5. System Workflow

Start
↓
Customer Enters Restaurant
↓
Add Customer to Queue
↓
Update Crowd Count in Hash Map
↓
Store Historical Data in BST
↓
Analyze Trends Using Arrays
↓
Predict Peak / Off-Peak Periods
↓
Notify Restaurant Management
↓
End

6. Space and Time Efficiency

Time Complexity

  • Queue operations: O(1)
  • Hash Map update/lookup: O(1) average
  • BST insertion/search: O(log N) (balanced)
  • Trend analysis using arrays: O(N)

Space Complexity

  • Queue storage: O(N)
  • Hash Map for time slots: O(T)
  • BST storage: O(N)
  • Arrays for trend data: O(N)

7. Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Alignment

  • SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth
  • SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
  • SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

8. Conclusion

The Restaurant Crowd & Demand Prediction System enables restaurants to make informed and proactive decisions by combining real-time monitoring with historical trend analysis. Using efficient algorithms and data structures, the system improves service quality, reduces food wastage, and enhances overall operational efficiency.
